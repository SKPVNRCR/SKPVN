We use GitHub Issues to collect submissions. This method is free, secure, and works perfectly with our GitHub Pages site.

Open Submission Form View All Submissions
How publishing works:

When you submit an article or poem, it appears as a GitHub issue labelled pending. An administrator will review your submission and, if approved, add the label approved. Once approved, your work will be published automatically on the Published Articles page.

  • A free GitHub account is required to submit.
  • No data is stored in your browser (no localStorage).
